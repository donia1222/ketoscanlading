Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: We Don't Store Anything

Your privacy in 4 clear points:

No user accounts created
No personal data requested (name, email, phone)
No behavioral analytics or advertising
No data sales - One-time payment

Everything remains on your device. The app is completely anonymous.

Data Storage

100% Local Storage

Your scan history, favorites and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this information.

No User Accounts

Keto Scanner works without creating user accounts. We don't collect or store personal identification information.

Nutritional Data

Open Food Facts

Nutritional data comes from Open Food Facts, a public and collaborative database. When scanning a product, only the barcode is sent to retrieve nutritional information.

Anonymous Processing

API queries are completely anonymous. No information that could identify you is included.

Business Model

One-Time Payment

Keto Scanner is paid once at download. No subscriptions, in-app purchases, or advertising. All features are available forever.

No Data Monetization

We don't sell, share or monetize any data. Your information never leaves your device.

App Permissions

Camera

Only used for scanning barcodes. No photos or videos are stored. Camera access is only during active scanning.

Internet

Internet connection is only required to query product nutritional information from Open Food Facts.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: info@lweb.ch
